Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when setting up an account or connecting integrations. This includes your business name, email address, physical storefront address, and OAuth tokens generated when you authenticate platforms like Google Business Profile or Trustpilot.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected information to sync incoming reviews from your selected locations, generate AI reply drafts for your review, and publish your approved responses back to the respective platforms.

3. OAuth Security & Google Data

Respondo's use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. We do not sell, rent, or share your Google OAuth tokens or profile details with third-party advertisers. All tokens are encrypted at rest.

4. Data Deletion

You may disconnect integrations at any time from your settings panel, which deletes the associated listing from our databases. Deleting your Respondo account permanently clears all stored credentials, tokens, and fetched reviews.
